About Us

The Einhorn Law Firm is a solo law practice run and operated by Matthew Einhorn, Esq.

Whether you are an individual or a Fortune 500 company, the Einhorn Law Firm utilizes our expert knowledge and experience to help you find solutions to your most pressing legal problems.

The Einhorn Law Firm provides legal representation for a variety of legal needs.

Estate Planning

Whether you are just beginning your estate planning, or you are in the process of changing or updating your estate plan, the Einhorn Law Firm can help.

  • -- Wills
  • -- Trusts
  • -- Probate
  • -- Probate Litigation

Asset Protection

Asset Protection is one of the least understood fields of law. If you have assets, and you want to protect them, let the Einhorn Law Firm assist you.

  • -- Operating Agreements

Civil Litigation

If you are being sued, or if you are thinking about initiating litigation, the Einhorn Law Firm can handle any phase of your civil litigation needs.

  • -- Discovery

  • -- Mediation & Arbitration

Immigration Law

Immigration Law is a complex field. Whether you, a family member, or your business needs assistance, the Einhorn Law Firm is here to help.
